At Re-Write, we believe in the essence of simplicity.

Our pens are crafted to let the natural beauty of wood shine through, allowing its unique grain and texture to tell its own story.

We handpick wood from various sources, it could be from a fallen tree after a storm, exotic pieces tucked away in attics or forgotten and broken furniture, that is why each pen signifies more than just history; it's about giving them a second life.

Our pens are refillable and built to last a lifetime, embodying our commitment to quality and sustainability.
